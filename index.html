About the Workshop

PixelPals is a free 12-day game development workshop that teaches students (ages 10–16) to design and build games using GDevelop. No prior experience required — we focus on design, problem solving, and creative expression.

Students10
Hours24
Games20

How it worked

Day 1–2: Game design basics & sprites
Day 3–6: Build core platformer mechanics
Day 7–8: Rock-Paper-Scissors game
Day 9–10: Level design & customization
Day 11–12: Playtesting & publishing
